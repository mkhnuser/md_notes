## How do you create a draft?

1. Choose a topic and a title of your draft;
2. Obtain as much info as possible on the given topic;
3. Test the idea against the people around you;
4. You are the integral part of your content;

        That's the reason why your content is interesting.

5. Use the STAR framework to express your idea.

Here are the types of articles one can create:

1. Technical article.
2. Explanation of some hard topic.
3. Controversial topics: something that divides the people in two groups.

## Example of a Draft

Suppose you are writing an article with the title JS from the Python Programmer Perspective:
Here is an example of how your draft might look:

```
JS from the Python Programmer Perspective

Python and JS are similar;
At the same time, they are different.
Tell about end-to-end ability of JS.
Tell about JS quirks.
Tell about how Python solves these quirks.
Conclude by showing how powerful JS is.
```

Each topic can be further break down and expanded during the next draft iteration.
