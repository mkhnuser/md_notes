# Social Media

## Outline

* Ideas;
* Draft;
* An article;
* Video Content;
* Content Distribution.

## Begin at the Beginning

Start with writing since it's the most convertible skill.
You can start with text-based platforms which have feed.

## Plan for a better writing

1. Choose the topic of your digital avatar: computer science, travelling, etc;
2. Brainstorm ideas: brainstorming has to happens as fast as possible;

        Try any 10-20 ideas - just spit them out on paper!

3. Choose three of the most interesting topics;
4. Write a draft: it has to be written as fast as possible;

        Just spit out sentances on paper without much thinking!

5. Structure your text and expand your draft.
6. Make a final version; prefer concise writing and clear language.

## Important

Understand: there is always a group of people who are hostile to your openness.
You don't have to be unique.
Iterate as fast as possible, in general.
