## Ideas and how do you get them?

Openness is extremely important: accept that you will be vulnerable,
but don't fear it; embrace it.

You've been programmed to be not creative and shut the f up;
your routine consists of habits which are detrimental to your brain.
It's extremely hard to concentrate and create: you have to be prepared for it.

While working on an idea, ask yourself:

        What does your reader / viewer get from your idea?
