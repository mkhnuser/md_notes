# Human Progress

## What is human progress?

### The definition of progress

People define progress differently.

For example, for environmentalists,
the progress is biocentric, meaning you must preserve the Earth,
whereas post-development people are concerned with preserving local indigenous groups.

## Evidence of progress

If we were to plot the amount of wealth, it would resemble a hockey stick:
most of the growth has happened in the last 200 years.
Understand: poverty can be relative and absolute.

### Data Resources

[Our world in data](https://ourworldindata.org/) is a resource for examination.
[Gap Minder](https://www.gapminder.org/) is another resource for data examination.

## Further Evidence of progress

Generally, the humanity improves in all areas except for terror, conflicts, and fish stock.
